Spicy Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, and sauté for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  5. Stir in the tomato puree and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add coconut milk and salt, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
