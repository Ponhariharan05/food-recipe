egg Briyani

Ingredients:

For Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • Salt to taste

For Egg Gravy:

  • 4-6 boiled eggs, peeled
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnishing

For Layering:

  • 1/4 cup fried onions (optional)
  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter
  • 1/4 cup milk with saffron (optional, for color and fragrance)
