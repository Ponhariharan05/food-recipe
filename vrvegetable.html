Vegetable Stir-fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli, carrots, and bell pepper. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add zucchini and continue cooking for another 2 minutes.
  5. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil in a small bowl.
  6. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir well to coat evenly.
  7. Cook for an additional 2 minutes until vegetables are tender but crisp.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot as a side dish or over steamed rice.
