Eggless cholatecake

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the powdered sugar to the dry ingredients and mix well.
  4. In another bowl, combine the milk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and vinegar. Stir well.
  5. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until smooth and combined. Do not overmix.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and spread it evenly.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Optional: Chocolate Frosting

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions for Frosting:

  1. In a bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.
  2. Add the powdered sugar and cocoa powder, and beat until smooth.
  3. Slowly add the milk, one tablespoon at a time, until you achieve a smooth and spreadable consistency.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Spread the frosting over the cooled cake.
