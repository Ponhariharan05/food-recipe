Tomato Bath

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice (washed and soaked for 15 minutes)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup peas (optional)
  • 2-3 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter for a few seconds.
  2. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes soften and the oil starts to separate.
  5. Stir in the soaked rice and peas (if using), mixing well with the tomato mixture.
  6. Pour in 2 cups of water and bring it to a boil. Once it boils, reduce the heat, cover the pan, and cook for 10-12 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked and the water is absorbed.
  7. Once done, fluff the rice gently with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with yogurt or raita.
