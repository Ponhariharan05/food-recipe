Paneer Recipe

Ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Stir well.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  6. Stir in fresh cream and mix well.
  7. Add paneer cubes, salt, and garam masala. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Serving Suggestions:

Serve hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice for a rich and delicious meal.
