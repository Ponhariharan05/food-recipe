Malai kofto Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For Kofta:
    • 2 cups grated paneer (cottage cheese)
    • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
    • 2 tablespoons cornflour
    • 2 tablespoons chopped cashews and raisins
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for deep frying
  • For Gravy:
    • 2 onions, finely chopped
    • 3 tomatoes, pureed
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1/2 cup fresh cream
    • 2 tablespoons cashew paste
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Kofta:
    1. In a bowl, mix grated paneer, mashed potatoes, cornflour, and salt. Knead into a smooth dough.
    2. Divide the mixture into small portions and stuff each with a little chopped cashews and raisins.
    3. Shape into smooth balls and deep fry until golden brown. Drain on paper towels and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Gravy:
    1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
    2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
    3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
    4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and tomato puree. Cook until the oil separates.
    5. Add cashew paste, salt, and a cup of water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
    6. Stir in fresh cream and garam masala. Mix well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Assemble:
    1. Place the koftas in the gravy and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
    2. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  4. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
