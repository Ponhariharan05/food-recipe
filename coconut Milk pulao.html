coconut milk pulao

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice (washed and soaked for 15-20 minutes)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 1-2 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pressure cooker or large pan. Add cumin seeds, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and bay leaf. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add the green chilies (if using), turmeric powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
  5. Add the soaked and drained rice to the pan and sauté for 2-3 minutes to coat the rice with the spices.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk and water, and add salt. Stir to combine.
  7. If using a pressure cooker, close the lid and cook for one whistle on medium heat. If using a regular pan, cover the pan and cook on low heat for 10-12 minutes or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  8. Once done, fluff the rice gently with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Serve the coconut milk pulao hot with raita, curry, or a side of your choice.
